Abstract

The *Educational Organization Using ServiceNow* project focuses on digitalizing and streamlining academic and administrative processes within an educational institution. Developed on the ServiceNow platform, this system integrates student, staff, and academic management functions into a single, unified environment. It automates manual tasks, enhances inter-departmental communication, and supports data-driven decision-making. By fostering transparency, accuracy, and sustainability, this project contributes to building a smarter and more connected educational ecosystem.

---

Objectives

* To digitalize and centralize student and staff records.
* To automate academic activities such as attendance, timetables, and performance tracking.
* To improve coordination and communication between departments using a shared digital platform.
* To ensure secure, reliable, and accessible management of institutional data.
* To promote eco-friendly, paperless administrative operations.



Project Modules

1. Student Management:
Stores and manages student profiles, attendance records, and academic performance details.

2. Staff Management:
Maintains staff information, schedules, and workload assignments.

3. Course Management:
Facilitates efficient creation, updating, and tracking of courses and syllabi.

4. Communication Portal:
Provides a digital space for notices, announcements, and feedback exchange.

5. Reports & Analytics:
Generates reports and insights to support academic planning and evaluation.

Conclusion

The Educational Organization Using ServiceNow project highlights how digital transformation can revolutionize institutional management. By integrating academic and administrative operations into a single platform, it reduces paperwork, improves communication, and enhances overall efficiency. This initiative encourages a modern, data-driven approach to education—creating a more organized, transparent, and technology-enabled learning environment.
